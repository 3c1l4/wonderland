Statistically speaking, He is one in eight billions,
(but we all know, statistics isn't real math).
So, if you really want to know
the authenticity of his brilliance,
observe that his glow
occurs in only one instance
every infinite number of numbers or so.

With the language of truth I am trying to convey,
although it is a working theory, I must say,
you have been part of the proof to me
that it is not so terrible to be.
