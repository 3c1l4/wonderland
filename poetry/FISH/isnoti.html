Is Not I

split imperfectly down the middle-most line,

Is and not are amidst this animated antithetical mess,
that Is Rightfully beget, but Not righteously sought.

through the trite turbulent ties of tight tongue twisted knots:
excessively fraught with rope-burned lies- 
Left and Right all riddled with rot!

Ruthlessly wrung, Not is stung

afraid, Not is now caught in a frayed knot Is did not knot— 
though Not thought Is knot intact this frayed knot,
Is only sought infact to fray knots it thought Not had not knot, 
so that Not would not get so caught in these knots
that Not thought Is had knot… 
as Is of course Not had ought.


Perhaps only still silence will wrought
to banish these loud knotted thoughts 
and let loose the noose Not accidentally knot
to finish this fight that Is never not Not forgot.
But, my oh my... who started this fickle mind fuck of a fight 
just to determine who might have a little more Right?
“Is Not I!” — in unison they thought…

My oh my! Is and Not have been freed from their bind!
For, this, they may finally agree Is Not lie:
it was most certainly Is Not I started the fight that they fought.
