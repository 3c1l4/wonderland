addict

03/25 

 
They warn you not to kiss the devil 
for his lips may be far too sweet 
and make you overnight an addict
forevermore in pursuit of heat.
But they never warn you about the angel,
how subdued is her treat,
how slow her ice will freeze over hell
and take cold command of your hearts chilling beat.
