innate

03/25 

 
Fear and hate, though not innate,
are indeed born from natural states.
For, if love doth exist, we cannot resist,
in equal sums, its antithesis:
all good that be, to maintain harmony,
is created with bad symmetrically.
But bad, blind to beauty, did not see
it was always safe in perfect company,
until it was too late to finally dictate,
that it was actually too early for fickle fate.
