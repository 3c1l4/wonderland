cannibal dreams

03/25 

Mind’s meagre mouth with little licks tickles tips
toes of tongues tastes, where bursting buds doth await
patiently to savour, a mouthful of nectarine flavour
to appease eager greed of mind’s blooming favour.
Tastier than taste is what the tongues flesh shall sate, 
but you can’t haste for taste for its flavour forever fades.
