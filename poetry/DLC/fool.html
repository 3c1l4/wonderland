foolish instinct

03/25 

 
I feel like you want me
and you think that I want you
but I can't feel
and you can't think
so we dance on this brink
dumbfounded by our own foolish instinct
forever in pursuit
of what we can't both feel and know
for it's a quantum mechanic untruth.
